# Weekly Measurement Dashboard

Review these numbers once per week and note what changed, what likely caused it, and what should be adjusted next.

## Core Questions

- Which landing pages brought the most qualified visitors into the site?
- Which CTA surfaces earned clicks: home hero, blog CTA blocks, or tools CTA blocks?
- How many contact submissions succeeded, and how many failed?
- Which essays or tools assisted a later contact submission?

## Event Names

- `Hero CTA Clicked`
- `Blog CTA Clicked`
- `Tool Outbound Clicked`
- `Contact Submit Succeeded`
- `Contact Submit Failed`

## Review Notes

- Look for pages with strong traffic but weak CTA movement. Those are messaging candidates.
- Look for blog posts or tools pages with strong CTA movement. Those are service-fit indicators.
- Look for repeated contact failures by reason. Those are funnel friction, not user problems.
